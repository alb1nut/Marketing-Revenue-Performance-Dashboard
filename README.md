# Marketing & Revenue Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

This project delivers an end-to-end **Business Intelligence solution** designed to analyze marketing effectiveness, customer behavior, and revenue performance for a multi-location service business.

The dashboard integrates data from **advertising campaigns, website sessions, and paid bookings** to provide stakeholders with a clear view of how marketing spend translates into website engagement, calls, and revenue.

---

## Business Objective

- Evaluate the performance of multiple marketing campaigns  
- Understand the customer journey from **ad click → website visit → call → paid order**  
- Identify high-performing locations and seasonal revenue trends  
- Support data-driven decisions to improve **ROI, conversion rates, and resource allocation**

---

## Data Overview

The analysis is based on structured datasets covering:

- **Advertising data**: impressions, clicks, and marketing spend by campaign  
- **Website session data**: user visits, calls, campaign attribution, and location  
- **Orders data**: paid bookings, revenue, hours booked, and court utilization  

**Time period:** 2018–2019

---

## Key KPIs & Metrics

- Total Revenue  
- Marketing Cost  
- Paid Orders  
- Website Visitors  
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)  
- Call Conversion Rate  
- Court Utilization  
- Revenue & Orders by Campaign  
- Monthly Revenue & Order Trends  
- Performance by Location  

---

## Dashboard Pages

- **Overview** – High-level business KPIs and performance summary  
- **Revenue Analysis** – Revenue trends, campaign performance, and marketing cost analysis  
- **Orders Analysis** – Orders volume, conversion rates, and utilization metrics  
- **Website Visit Analysis** – Website traffic, calls, and campaign attribution  

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI** (Power Query, DAX, Data Modeling)  
- **Excel / CSV data sources**

---

## Key Insights

- Identified top-performing marketing campaigns based on revenue and conversion efficiency  
- Highlighted locations contributing the highest revenue and booking volume  
- Revealed seasonal patterns in revenue and customer demand  
- Exposed gaps between marketing spend and conversion performance  

---

## How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file  
2. Open with **Power BI Desktop**  
3. Interact with filters for **campaigns, time periods, and locations**
